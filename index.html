ABOUT US

Kautilya International Foundation

India has been the fountain head for philosophical, cultural and civilizational research for the world. The research history of Bharat (India) has not only been studied within the country but the whole world acknowledges It’s contributions in many fields. The Kautilya International Foundation (KIF) is a platform that connects researchers of the globe to discourse various critical contributions of India to the world society. The KIF is aimed to create a comprehensive knowledge repository of Historical facts of Indian Business History; Folk and Culture Heritage; India’s contributions to Mathematics; Language Science and Dialects; Indian Science and Technology; Concepts of Indian Public Policy Mechanism; Teaching tools and knowledge management techniques that can guide the directions of India centric research globally in order to create humanitarian thoughts, socially grounded theories and inclusivity in the global society.

The KIF will develop a pool of globally recognized researchers who can act as though leaders and drive the research for global peace by following Indian philosophical theories and concepts. This foundation will bring-out research outputs periodically based on various activities like seminars, colloquiums, conferences, magazines, bulletins, newsletters, monographs, working papers and book volumes.

In larger picture the KIF will aim to create a thought forum for the global researchers to investigate and understand the India’s contributions to world
